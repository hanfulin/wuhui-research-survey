### 中文摘要（v2.2 · 结构式 · 已定稿）

> **目的** 梳理公立医院科研经费包干制研究进展，识别内部控制视角下的研究共识、分歧与空白。**方法** 以 24 份政策文件、理论框架与学术文献为基础，构建"科研项目全生命周期×COSO 五要素"双维矩阵，对相关研究进行归类与比较分析。**结果** 既有研究在执行环节、控制活动与信息化嵌入方面较为集中，在申报前端干预和成果转化后端控制方面相对薄弱；学界已在放控并行、负面清单和信息化嵌入等方向形成共识，但在过程管理与包干制兼容、分级授权阈值、绩效考核与内控指标耦合等方面仍缺乏可操作方案。**结论** 公立医院科研经费包干制落地的关键，不是简单减少审批，而是在放权基础上重构内部控制体系，并以负面清单、分级授权和嵌入式系统形成可执行的中观工具。

**关键词**：公立医院；科研经费包干制；内部控制；负面清单；全生命周期管理

### 英文摘要 / Abstract（v2.2 · 结构式 · 待通讯作者把关）

> **Abstract** **Objective** To review research progress on the lump-sum system for research funding in public hospitals and identify the consensus, disagreements and gaps from the perspective of internal control. **Methods** Based on 24 policy documents, theoretical frameworks and academic publications, a two-dimensional matrix of "research-project full lifecycle × COSO five components" was constructed to classify and compare the existing literature. **Results** Current studies are concentrated in the execution stage, control activities and information-system embedding, while insufficient attention has been paid to front-end intervention at the application stage and back-end control over achievement transformation. Consensus has been reached on parallel decentralization and regulation, negative-list management and IT-embedded controls. However, operational solutions remain inadequate in terms of compatibility between process management and the lump-sum system, thresholds for tiered authorization, and the coupling between performance assessment and internal-control indicators. **Conclusion** The implementation of the lump-sum system in public hospitals should not be understood as a simple reduction of approval procedures. Its key lies in redesigning internal control after decentralization and developing operational tools including negative lists, tiered authorization and embedded information systems.

**Keywords**: public hospitals; research-funding lump-sum system; internal control; negative list; full-lifecycle management

### 英文摘要起草说明（供通讯作者审校时参考）

- **结构式四段**：v2 改为 Objective / Methods / Results / Conclusion，对齐《现代医院》近两年论著模板；不再用整段式
- **关键术语处理**：
  - "包干制" → `lump-sum system (Bao-Gan-Zhi)` 首次出现可给拼音，后续直接用 lump-sum system（v2.2 当前正文未给拼音以节省字数，是否补由通讯作者定）
  - "放权" → `decentralize/decentralization`（不译 delegate，因 delegation 暗含上下级授权关系，与改革取向不符）
  - "嵌入式系统" → `embedded information systems`（IT-embedded controls 用于解释，避免与硬件 embedded systems 混淆）
  - "负面清单" → `negative list / negative-list management`（治理研究常见译法）
  - "分级授权" → `tiered authorization`（保留学术界对 macro/meso/micro 三分法的层级表达逻辑）
  - "约束工具 → 落地支撑" → 当前 v2.2 英文摘要未直接出现该范式标签（中文摘要也省略以保持结构式简洁），但正文 Conclusion 段对应表述见综述第 8 节
- **可调点**：当前 ~225 词，结构式四段都已自然展开；通讯作者若觉某段过简可在 Results 段加 1–2 句具体数字（如"5×5 matrix mapping 24 entries; 7 papers concentrated on execution × control activities"）
- **降低 AI 味**：避免 "In this paper, we"、"It is worth noting that"、"Furthermore" 等模板词；首次术语用斜体 + 中文术语首现解释，体现中文核心期刊语感
