## 块 6：在中文摘要后追加英文摘要

**Word 里找**：你的中文【摘要】+【关键词】那两段（结尾是"全生命周期管理"）

**在【关键词】下方另起一行，新增以下两段**：

```
Abstract  Against the backdrop of China's reform that decentralizes research-funding authority and the parallel pursuit of high-quality development in public hospitals, the implementation of the research-funding "lump-sum system" (Bao-Gan-Zhi) in public hospitals has become a critical agenda for internal-control construction. Drawing on 22 policy documents and academic publications, this paper adopts a two-dimensional matrix of "research-project full lifecycle × COSO five components" as its analytical framework to systematically map the research landscape, surface the consensus, and identify the disagreements surrounding the lump-sum system in public hospitals. The review finds that the existing literature clusters densely in two zones—the execution stage and the embedding of information systems—while leaving substantial gaps at the front end (application and intervention) and the back end (achievement transformation). Scholars have reached consensus on parallel decentralization-and-regulation, IT-embedded controls, and the direction of a negative list, yet systematic gaps remain at the meso-operational level, including the compatibility between full-process management and the lump-sum system, the thresholds for tiered authorization, and the coupling between performance management and internal control. We argue that the implementation challenge of the lump-sum system in public hospitals is, in essence, a question of how internal control should be redesigned after decentralization; internal control thereby shifts from a "constraint tool" to an "implementation enabler", and the parallel deployment of "negative list + tiered authorization + embedded systems" emerges as a plausible meso-level toolkit.

Key words  public hospitals; research-funding lump-sum system; internal control; negative list; full-lifecycle management
```

> 字数：约 230 词，落在《现代医院》英文摘要常规 200–280 词区间。
> Bao-Gan-Zhi 拼音首现按学术惯例**斜体**（Word 选中后按 Ctrl+I），后文出现 lump-sum system 即可。
> 通讯作者把关后可微调；常见可调点是把"meso-operational level"换成更易懂的"operational"、或在"22 policy documents and academic publications"后加 "(2012–2026)" 强化时间跨度。

1. **正文里的引用编号是否还对得上？** —— 5-30 v1 是 22 条，与 5-19 旧版 16 条编号不一一对应。Word 正文用 Ctrl+F 把每个 [n] 都过一遍，确认指向的是正确文献
